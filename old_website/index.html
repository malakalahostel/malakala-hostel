Facilities

Library(Vidyodaya Sangha)

The library helps the students to have reference with different books related to their academics.There are more than 3000 books in the library.There are so many books which help the students to score good marks in their academics.

Sports

  • Table Tennis

    Events

    Independence Day

    It is celebrated every year on 15th August.
    There will be flaghoisting and speeches given by students in the prsence of respected Donors.

    Ganesh Chathurthi

    Every year Ganesh Chathurthi is celebrated by keeping Ganesh idol. Donors and all the old boarders of the hostel will be invited.It will be celebrated in a grand fashion.

    Republic Day

    Every year Republic Day is celebrated on 26th January. Our hostel will also organize free Eye-checkup,Dental checkup and blood donation.

    Macrito

    Malakala Hostel hosts Cricket tournment every year where different teams compitite each other.

    Hostel Day

    Every year hostel day will be celebrated in a grand manner. It will be celebrated in Malakala Convention Center. All donors and Old boarders will be invited to the aspicious day.

    Smiriti

    Malakala Hostel host indoor games such as table tennis,carrom,chess,treasure hunt and many more games which will be played by different hostels.
